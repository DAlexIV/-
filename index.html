Notes

  • General disclaimer about countries US relocation process is tough and long, Europe, UK, Canada, UAE and Asia (Singapore, Thailand) are more straight-forward and fast. If you want to leave as soon as possible, non-visa countries like Turkey, Georgia, *stans and Armenia should be your targets
  • Czech Republic and closed work visas for Russian nationals. Estonia closed work visas for Russian and Belorussian nationals.
  • means that company doesn’t hire to US directly from Russia, but intra-company transfer is possible (L1 visa). That's usually true for FAANG companies
  • Hiring slowdown - some companies continues hiring only for essential roles.
  • Referral - this is not a recruiter. They won't search positions for you or provide any career consulting. Search for a position on the website first, prepare your CV and *only then* write to the people on the list with a link to a particular position.
  • Don't apply on the website Please wait before you will be referred.
  • When writing to a contact please be polite, introduce yourself, let them know how you learnt about them and only then ask if they can refer you. They might reject in case your profile doesn't match the position - don't take it personally and continue your search with other resources.
  • H1-B visas are distributed in the March and April of every year. Some companies hold US hiring from April to December to target H1-B lottery.
